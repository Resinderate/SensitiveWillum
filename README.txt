SensitiveWillum is a 2D platforming game written in C# / XNA.

You take on the role of Willum, where you must keep away from
all the sticky creatures of the game, collect all that you need and
reach the end of the level to your freedom.

Released on IndieDB where you can see demo videos / pictures.
http://www.indiedb.com/games/sensitivewillum
